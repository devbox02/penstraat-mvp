RESTAURANT SEASONS

International Flavours

At Seasons, we’re firm believers in making everyday eating a little bit extraordinary. We’re one part local, one part global, and one part specialty, infusing fresh quality produce with far-flung ideas to create dishes which will often surprise and always delight. Our menu changes with the seasons, inspired by the abundance and variety of edible offerings from soil & sea. We’re less about fine dining and more about fun dining, and like to make food you can share, food you can talk about, food you’ll want more of, and food you’ll come back for.

Returning 'home' to the Penstraat in September of 2016 after a 2 year absence - a great addition to the already blossoming Penstraat. Restaurant 'Seasons' has a very international menu with serves delicacies from the Caribbean, but also French Gooseliver, snails and tournedos, Dutch mussels and oysters, Italian pastas and jumbo prawns, some Asian plates and plenty of beautiful fish and lobster courses. Let Chef Michael and his brother Dave take you on a culinary journey. You won't be dissappointed. Reservations is highly recommended.
