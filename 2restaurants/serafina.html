RESTAURANT SERAFINA

Classic Italian Cuisine

Old school classic Italian restaurants have been serving impeccable food while simultaneously making their guests feel like a member of their own family for centuries - and Restaurant Serafina is no different. Serafina is a classic Italian restaurant offering an excellent combination of fresh dishes, excellent wines and service that is carefully overseen by the manager and owner, Maurice. Indulge in classic Italian dishes or try some of our homemade pasta specials. Don't forget to make a reservation!
