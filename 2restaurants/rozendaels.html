RESTAURANT ROZENDAELS

Brasserie / International Flavours

This is one restaurant that will leave you wanting to return sooner rather than later. International flair and Dutch sobriety are intertwined in the menu as well as the care-free ambiance, making it a unique experience. Executive chef Danny, a Dutch native with decades of restaurant experience, delivers international cuisine to the developing neighborhood, perfectly complemented by a globally sourced wine list. An upscale dining destination revitalizing the Penstraat neighborhood, an international style brasserie with a Caribbean flair.
