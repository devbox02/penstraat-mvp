RESTAURANT BELLE TERRACE

Flavours ot the Americas & Caribbean

Avila’s Belle Terrace is a unique open-air location with an exceptional classic atmosphere. Seated underneath the spectacular Flamboyant tree, you do not only taste the delicious dishes but, also the traditional atmosphere of the Avila Beach Hotel. The restaurant features an exquisite à la carte menu in the evening where you can taste everything that our region has to offer. The Belle Terrace on Curacao is open daily for lunch from noon to 2.30 pm and for dinner from Monday till Saturday from 6.30 to 10.00 pm.
